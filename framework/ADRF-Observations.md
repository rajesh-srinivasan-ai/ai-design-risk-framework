# ADRF Observations

This document captures recurring observations that emerge during the evolution of ADRF.

Observations are not principles, risk categories, or formal guidance. They represent patterns and insights that repeatedly surface during the study of AI design risks.

---

## ADRF-O1

**When transparency is hidden, risk evolves naturally.**

As transparency decreases, understanding becomes more difficult. When users, operators, builders, or governance bodies cannot adequately understand system behavior, assumptions, limitations, or outcomes, risk can emerge, accumulate, and remain difficult to recognize.

This observation serves as an ongoing lens through which AI system design risks may be examined.
